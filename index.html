Touchpoint: Hope

Why should I trust God as my hope?

We can trust God because His character is unchanging and His promises are secure. In times of trouble, He is a refuge. The scriptures show us that hope in God does not disappoint. True hope is not just a wish; it is a confident expectation of God's goodness.
